Today was the big hiking day. First hike of the day was [Mosaic Canyon](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/mosaic-canyon-trail). Very beautiful, like being on the moon with the black gravel. Second hike was [Golden Canyon](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/golden-canyon-trail-and-badlands-loop-to-zabriskie-point). This one was the bee's knees. Rock slabs were incredible. The last hike of the day was the [Mesquite Flat Sand Dunes](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/mesquite-flat-sand-dunes-trail) right before sunset.
